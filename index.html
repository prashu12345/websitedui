Temporal Analysis

The Temporal Analysis examines alcohol-impaired crash trends over various timeframes, including yearly, monthly, daily, and hourly breakdowns. The data highlights consistent issues during late-night hours and weekends, reinforcing the need for targeted interventions during high-risk periods. Year-over-year comparisons reveal a positive reduction in crashes, suggesting improvements in awareness and law enforcement.
